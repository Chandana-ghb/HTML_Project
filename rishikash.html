Rishikesh is a sacred city located in the Dehradun district of Uttarakhand, India, on the banks of the Ganga River, at the foothills of the Himalayas. Known as the "Yoga Capital of the World", it is a major center for spirituality, meditation, and wellness. The city attracts visitors from around the globe who come to learn yoga, attend ashrams, and take part in spiritual retreats.
