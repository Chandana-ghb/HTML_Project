Baga Beach

Baga Beach, located in North Goa, is one of the most popular and vibrant beaches in the state, known for its lively atmosphere, thrilling water sports, and bustling nightlife. Stretching along the Arabian Sea, Baga attracts tourists with its soft golden sands, swaying palm trees, and the scenic Baga River flowing into the sea.
